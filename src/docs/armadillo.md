Hello everyone, 

this is Štěpán Váňa, founder and leader of Project Armadillo. I would like to introduce you to my project, which I want to dedicate myself to not only as a student but also as an ordinary person from a moral standpoint.

For a long time now, I have been observing how artificial intelligence is expanding into all corners of the world, reaching adults, young people, and the elderly alike. This in itself would be absolutely fine, but every current AI model faces one major issue—it is easily exploitable and it hallucinates. Since 2022, the prevalence of Large Language Models (LLMs) has increased enormously.

### Our Mission

The arrival of artificial intelligence to the wider public, as I have already mentioned, has brought a lot of good, but also a lot of bad. With Project Armadillo, which is based on the research and development of artificial general intelligence, I want to take the next stage of AI into my own hands and oversee its safe development in the simplest and most secure way possible. The project's goal is not to oversee the global development of artificial general intelligence. Its mission is to develop a safe and fully controlled form of AGI.

### The Challenges

I realize that we, as the general public, do not yet have a very clear picture of AGI model development, which is why I want to publish updates and development reports from the project right here on this website.
As humans, we do not yet have the means to develop and operate a large-scale AGI model, because such a model can grow to dimensions in terms of data and computing power that we cannot even imagine.

The development of AGI will be another giant leap for humanity, and that is why we want to ensure this step is a safe one.
